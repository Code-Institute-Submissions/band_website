Oompah Brass

The U.K's Original Rock 'n' Roll Oompah Band

We are Oompah Brass

“Legends in Lederhosen!”

Chris Evans BBC Radio 2

Oompah Brass are a five-piece brass group specialising in virtuosic and high-energy performances of rock and pop classics.

Formed back in 2006, Oompah Brass began as a traditional Bavarian Oompah band. These days however, you won't hear any German marches or Polkas - they may still be clad in lederhosen but their repertoire of ‘oompah' music is drawn from the likes of AC/DC, Queen, Bon Jovi, Led Zeppelin, Taylor Swift and Katy Perry to name just a few!

Oompah Brass are the resident band at two of London's coolest bierkellers - Katzenjammers, and the Octoberfest Pub.

Check when we are playing and come down and say hi!
